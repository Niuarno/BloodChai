How BloodChai Works

A complete guide to our platform

🩸 For Blood Donors

1
Register with your blood type, district, and contact info
2
Set your active status to appear in donor searches
3
Receive blood requests on your dashboard
4
Accept or decline requests. When you donate, your record is updated automatically
5
Wait at least 90 days between donations for safety

🏥 For Blood Recipients

1
Go to Find Donor and filter by district and blood type
2
Browse available donors (names and photos shown, contact info protected)
3
Click "Request Blood" and fill the contact form
4
The donor receives your request and can contact you directly

📋 Donation Rules (Bangladesh)

✅ Must be 18–60 years old
✅ Weight at least 50 kg
✅ Hemoglobin ≥ 12.5 g/dL
✅ 90 days between donations
❌ No fever or illness
❌ No recent surgery
